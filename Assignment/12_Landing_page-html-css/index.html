About Me

I am a graduate of the Art History department at Ege University. After completing my undergraduate studies, I pursued a master's degree in Art History and Enrichment of Cultural Heritage at the University of Genoa in Italy.

During my master's program, I conducted thesis research in collaboration with the University of Cologne, spending six months in Cologne, Germany. I have been living in Italy/Genoa for nearly four years, where I have developed a keen interest in UX&UI design. Over the past two years, I have worked on various projects in this field. Additionally, I am involved in 3D modeling and use 3D Max, Blender and unreal engine 5 (landscape-blueprints) for my projects.

I love creating and designing new things, and combining art with design to produce creative and effective solutions is a great passion of mine. With my deep knowledge of art history and cultural heritage, along with my experience in design, I aim to advance further in both my academic and professional career. My goal is to share this knowledge and passion with a broader audience.
